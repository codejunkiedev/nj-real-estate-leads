Questions we hear a lot

Answered before you ask.

How is this different from Zillow Premier Agent?
Zillow sends the same lead to 4–5 agents simultaneously. You compete on response speed, not relationship. Our leads go to one agent per territory — full stop. Different product entirely.
How do you actually get these leads? Is this legal?
Every signal is public record — filed with a NJ county court or municipal tax office. Lis Pendens, Sheriff Sales, and Tax Liens are matters of public court record that any attorney, PI, or title company can access. Skip tracing is done through licensed data providers (IDI Core, BatchData) — the same tools used by private investigators. On your end, standard real estate cold-outreach rules apply.
What does “qualified listing appointment” mean in the guarantee?
A face-to-face or video meeting scheduled with a property owner from our batch who is actively considering selling. Not a callback. Not a text exchange. An appointment on your calendar. That’s the bar.
What if my territory is already locked by another agent?
We check live on the call. If your primary zip is taken, you go on the waitlist at no charge with first right of refusal if that agent cancels. We’ll also tell you which adjacent zips are still open so you can consider a territory shift.
Why is there a setup fee for new counties?
Setup only applies if you’re the first agent signing up in a county we don’t yet cover. Building out a new county is 3–4 weeks of real work — court portal integration, VA training, initial verification cycles. The $2,500 is credited against your first two months of service, so you’re pre-paying, not paying extra. Agents in counties we already cover see no setup fee.
What’s the actual time commitment from me?
Monday morning: review your batch, call Hot leads first (aim for 48-hour response). That’s the system. The leads are finished deliverables — scored, scripted, and skip-traced — so there’s no research work on your side. Most partners spend 30–60 minutes working a weekly batch.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Month to month, no annual contract. Cancel any billing cycle. Your territory releases back to the pool after 30 days. If you come back later, we can’t guarantee it’ll still be available.
